Wern & her family's favourite sambal

"In my home in Malaysia, sambal isn't just a sauce — it's the heartbeat of every meal."

How to sambal

One spoon, a hundred dinners.

01

Drizzle

A tablespoon over a fried, scrambled, or boiled egg. Instant breakfast upgrade.

02

Mix

Stir 1–2 tbsp into steaming hot rice or noodles. Or stir-fry it with grains for a charred kick.

03

Stir-fry

Drop into hot oil, sauté 30 seconds until fragrant, then toss in protein or veggies.

04

Dip

Spoon onto fried chicken, satay, or anything that needs a serious spicy kick.
